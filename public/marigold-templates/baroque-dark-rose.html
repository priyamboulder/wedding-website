our story

We met during junior year at Michigan — both reaching for the same dog-eared Rushdie novel in Hatcher Library. What started as study sessions turned into late-night walks, turned into chai at midnight, turned into years of showing up for each other. On the rooftop of a crumbling haveli at sunset in Rajasthan, Arjun asked. She said yes before he finished the sentence.
