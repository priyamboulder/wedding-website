our story
We met during junior year at Michigan — both reaching for the same dog-eared Rushdie novel in Hatcher Library. What started as study sessions turned into late-night walks, turned into chai at midnight, turned into years of showing up for each other across cities and time zones. On the rooftop of a crumbling haveli at sunset in Rajasthan, Arjun asked. She said yes before he finished the sentence. Then they both cried. Then they ordered samosas.

Check out these beautiful
things to do when you're
not celebrating with us
City Palace

The crown jewel of Udaipur — a sprawling complex of courtyards, towers, and terraces overlooking Lake Pichola. Allow at least two hours and wear comfortable shoes.

Lake Pichola Boat Ride

Take a sunset boat ride past the Lake Palace and Jag Mandir. The light on the water in November is extraordinary — bring your camera.

Jagdish Temple

A 17th-century temple in the heart of the old city, dedicated to Lord Vishnu. The carved marble facade is breathtaking. Early morning visits are the quietest.

Ambrai Ghat

The best sunset spot in Udaipur. Grab a table at Ambrai restaurant and watch the City Palace light up across the water. Arrive by 5pm for a good seat.
